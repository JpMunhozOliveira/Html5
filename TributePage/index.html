Who is Shigeru Miyamoto?

Shigeru Miyamoto is a Japanese game designer, widely regarded as one of the greatest names in the digital gaming industry.

Born in Sonobe, Japan, in 1952, Miyamoto joined Nintendo in 1977, where he began working on arcade games.

In 1981, Miyamoto created his first major success, the game "Donkey Kong," which was followed by other iconic games such as "Super Mario Bros." and "The Legend of Zelda."

Today, Miyamoto continues to work at Nintendo as a consultant and producer, helping to create new games and maintain the company's classic franchises.
